Jab Depression Ek Mask Pehen Leta Hai: 5 Baatein Jo Hum "Main Theek Nahi Hoon" Ki Jagah Kehte Hain

Depression Awareness Series — Part 3 | Ek Pharmacist Ki Nazar Se

Afreen ke dwara 6 min read Mental Health · Pharmacist Writes

Ek baar ek ladki meri pharmacy counter pe aayi.
Achhe kapde, dheemi awaaz, muskurati hui.
Usne mujhe antidepressant ka prescription diya aur ek dum casually bola, "Yeh bas neend ke liye hai."
Lekin yeh sirf neend ke liye nahi tha.

Yahi karta hai depression. Yeh humein sikhaa deta hai — khud ko chhota karo. Theek dikhte raho. Chahe kuch bhi theek na ho.

Part 1 mein humne un signs ko dekha jinhein hum personality samajh lete hain.
Part 2 mein humne un sentences ko dekha jo hum asli baat ki jagah keh dete hain.
Aaj — Part 3 mein — hum aur gehre jaate hain. Kyunki depression ko pehchaanna sirf aadha kaam hai. Mushkil aadha yeh samajhna hai ki yeh itne time tak chhupta kyun rehta hai.

5 Wajahein Jis Se Depression Chhupta Rehta Hai — Aur Kya Help Karta Hai

Wajah 01

Humein sikhaya gaya hai ki chup rehkar dard sahna hi strength hai

Zyaadatar Indian families mein emotional pain ek private cheez hai. Doosron ke saamne rote nahi. Yeh nahi kehte, "Main takleef mein hoon." Bas uthaye chalte hain. Aur jab itne time tak uthate ho, toh yeh uthana hi tumhari pehchaan ban jaata hai.

Lekin chup rehna strength nahi hai. Yeh bas ek achhe naam mein lipti hui akela hai.

Sach yeh hai

Madad maangna medically sabse sahi kaam hai jo aap kar sakte hain. Brain akele rehne se nahi, connection se jaldi theek hota hai. Yeh poetry nahi — yeh neuroscience hai.

Wajah 02

Hum "Depression" label se darte hain

Yeh word hi kisi judgment jaisa lagta hai. Jaise "Mujhe depression hai" kehna matlab kuch permanent hai. Kuch sharmnaak hai. Kuch jo tumhare saath hamesha rahega.

Lekin depression ek clinical diagnosis hai — character ka judgment nahi. Isse tum jo ho, usme kuch nahi badlata.

Sach yeh hai

Kisi condition ko naam dena uske ilaaj ka pehla kadam hai. Jis cheez ko aap accept karne se inkaar karte hain, usse aap theek nahi kar sakte.

Wajah 03

Hum "itna bura hone tak" wait karte hain

Hum khud se kehte hain: "Itna serious nahi hai. Main abhi bhi kaam kar raha hoon. Abhi ekdum bottom nahi chhua." Lekin depression ke liye care ka condition yeh nahi hai ki ekdum bottom tak pahuncho.

Aap dentist ke paas tab nahi jaate jab cavity root canal ban jaaye. Mental health bhi yahi early, preventive attention deserve karta hai.

Sach yeh hai

Mild symptoms ka jaldi ilaaj, late stage pe kiye severe ilaaj se kahin behtar results deta hai.

Wajah 04

Hum jaante hi nahi ki "help lena" kaisa dikhta hai

Bahut se log reach out karna chahte hain — par jaante nahi kahan se. Psychiatrist bahut serious lagta hai. Counsellor bahut mehenga. Family ko batana bahut complicated. Toh kuch nahi karte. Aur kuch na karna sabse mehenga option hai.

Sach yeh hai

Help lena utna chhota step ho sakta hai jitna ek helpline pe call karna. Apne doctor ko ek message type karna. Ek kadam. Bas ek.

Wajah 05

Hum darte hain ki andar jhanknein pe kya milega

Yeh sabse honest wajah hai. Agar main uss udaasi ko accept kar loon — kya hua agar yeh bahut badi ho? Lekin ulta sach hai. Depression andheron mein sabse bada hota hai. Yeh sisakta hai — dheere dheere, support ke saath — jab ise finally dekha jaata hai.

Sach yeh hai

Jo aap feel karte hain use acknowledge karne se aap toot nahi jaayenge. Pehli baar, aap ise heal hone ka mauka denge.
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Main sirf woh insaan nahi hoon jo tablets ginsti hai aur prescriptions padhti hai.

Main woh hoon jo medicine aur un palon ke beech khadi rehti hoon jab log apne words dhundh rahe hote hain.

Agar aap yeh padh rahe hain aur khud ko yahan dekh rahe hain — yeh koi coincidence nahi hai.

Depression treatable hai. Aap akele nahi hain. Aur yeh fact ki aap yeh padh rahe hain — iska bhi matlab hai.
