Woh Khamoshi Jo Maine Counter Ke Peeche Dekhi

A Pharmacist's Perspective on Depression — Jo Hum Dekhte Hain, Par Bolte Nahi

Kai baar koi aata hai prescription lekar.

Haath thoda kaanpte hain. Aankhein thaki hui. "Bhaiya ne bheja hai" — yeh kehkar rukh jaate hain ek pal, jaise kuch aur kehna chahte hain.

Main dawaai deti hun. Dosage samjhati hun. Woh chale jaate hain.

Par woh jo unsaid tha — woh counter pe reh jaata hai.

Pharmacist hone ka matlab sirf dawa dena nahi hota.

Humari training mein molecules hote hain, drug interactions hote hain, contraindications hote hain. Par koi nahi sikhata ke jab koi Sertraline ki pehli strip lete hue rota ho — toh kya bolein.

Aur main yeh isliye nahi likh rahi ke main koi hero hun. Main isliye likh rahi hun kyunki mujhe bahut der lagi yeh samajhne mein — ke woh dawa nahi, insaan tha.

Depression ka chehra woh nahi jo hum sochte hain.

Woh akela, andhere kamre mein baitha insaan nahi hota hamesha. Woh subah office jaata hai, bacchon ko school chhod ke aata hai, WhatsApp status pe muskurata hai — aur raat ko neend ki dawa maangta hai bina prescription ke.

Yahi woh log hain jo mere counter pe aate hain.

Ek din ek aunty aayi thi. Saath mein teen shopping bags. Lipstick fresh. Woh perfectly theek lag rahi thi.

Unhone ek parchi di — Escitalopram 10mg.

Phir dheere se bola, "Yeh kisi ko nahi batana beta."

Main ruk gayi. Woh ek dawaai nahi chhupaana chahti thi — woh apni takleef chhupaana chahti thi. Kyunki hamare yahan depression ko kamzori kehte hain.

Maine unhe dawaai di. Saath mein sirf itna kaha — "Aunty, yeh lena himmat ki nishani hai."

Unki aankhein bhar aayi.

Pharmacist ke paas ek unique position hoti hai. Doctor se zyada accessible. Therapist se zyada regular milna. Koi appointment nahi lagti.

Isliye hum woh log hote hain jinhein pehle pata chalta hai — ke kuch theek nahi hai. Yahi mujhe change karna hai. Apni writing se. Apni awaaz se.

Agar aap yeh padh rahe hain aur khud kuch aisa feel kar rahe hain — thakaan jo neend se nahi jaati, khushi jo reasons ke bawajood nahi aati — toh please:

Yeh kamzori nahi hai. Yeh brain chemistry hai. Yeh medical condition hai. Aur is ka ilaaj hota hai.

Main Afreen hun. Ek pharmacist. Aur ab ek writer.

Kyunki counter ke peeche jo mujhe dikhta hai — woh duniya ko dikhna chahiye.
