# 5 Signs of Depression Jo Hum "Bas Zindagi Hai" Bol Ke Ignore Kar Dete Hain

**Depression Awareness Series — Part 1 | Ek Pharmacist Ki Nazar Se**

---

Ek baar ek aadmi aaya meri pharmacy mein.
Muskura raha tha. Bol raha tha kaam theek hai, ghar mein sab achha hai.
Phir usne ek prescription thama — antidepressants ka.

Maine poocha — *"Kitne time se le rahe ho ye?"*

*"Ek saal,"* usne kaha. *"Par kisi ko pata nahi."*

Ek saal. Akele.

---

## 1. Aisi Thakaan Jo Neend Ke Baad Bhi Nahi Jaati

8 ghante so gaye. Phir bhi bed se uthna impossible lagta hai.

Yeh brain ki thakaan hai. Jab serotonin aur dopamine sahi se kaam karna band kar dete hain — body bina kisi wajah ke physically drained feel karne lagti hai.

> **Pharmacist ka note:** Yeh aalas nahi hai. Yeh neuroscience hai.

---

## 2. Jo Cheezein Achhi Lagti Theen — Ab Kuch Bhi Feel Nahi Hoti

Tumhari favourite show. Favourite khaana. Doston ke saath baithna. Sab kuch flat lagta hai. Jaise zindagi se taste hi chala gaya ho.

Isko kehte hain **Anhedonia** — depression ka sabse common aur sabse chhupa hua sign.

Khud se poochho: Kya ek bhi cheez hai jo pehle khushi deti thi — aur ab kuch nahi deti? Ek bhi? Dhyan do.

---

## 3. Chhoti Chhoti Baaton Pe Rona Ya Gussa Aa Jaana

Kuch chota sa hota hai — aur aankhein bhar aati hain. Ya gussa ek dum se bhadak jaata hai.

Log kehte hain *"Itne sensitive kyun ho?"* ya *"Drama band karo."*

Par yeh drama nahi hai. Yeh nervous system ka overload hai. Jab brain already struggle kar raha hota hai — toh chhoti cheezein bhi unbearable lagti hain.

> **Yaad rakho:** Zyada sensitivity ek sign hai. Weakness nahi.

---

## 4. Sabse Door Hone Lagna

WhatsApp message dikh raha hai. Reply nahi karte. Plans cancel ho rahe hain. Ghar mein rehna, logon ke saath rehne se zyada safe lagta hai.

Ghar wale sochte hain aap arrogant ho gaye. Dost sochte hain aap unse naraaz ho.

Par sach yeh hai — yeh withdrawal depression ke sabse common signs mein se ek hai. Brain itna overwhelmed ho jaata hai ki social interaction ek extra bojh lagta hai.

> **Pharmacist ka observation:** Jo log akele aate hain mere paas — aur yeh kehte hain ki ghar mein kisi ko pata nahi — yahi lete aate hain apne saath.

---

## 5. "Sab Theek Hai" — Par Andar Se Kuch Nahi

Yeh sabse khatarnaak sign hai.

Haste ho. Kaam karte ho. Bahar se sab normal lagta hai.

Par andar — ek khaalaapan hai. Zindagi ho rahi hai — par kuch feel nahi ho raha. Na khushi, na udaasi. Bas numbness.

Log is baare mein kabhi baat nahi karte — kyunki explain karna mushkil hai. *"Sab theek hai"* — yeh sabse bada jhooth hai jo hum khud se bolte hain.
